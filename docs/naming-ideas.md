# Domain Name Ideas

Candidate domains for the game (working title *Boiling Point*). Goals: **clever**,
**cheap** (lean toward low-cost TLDs — `.club`, `.xyz`, `.gg`, `.io`, `.fun`,
`.cards`, `.lol` — since exact-match `.com` is usually taken/expensive), and **good**
(memorable, on-theme: a boiling cauldron, hidden volatility, push-your-luck, betrayal).

> Availability and price change constantly — **verify at a registrar before committing**.
> `.club`/`.xyz` are highlighted because they're cheap and short single-word hacks are
> often still open.

## Top picks (short, on-theme, cheap TLD)

| Domain | Read | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **boiling.club** | "boiling club" | Single-word hack; the game's name, members-of-a-table vibe. Cheap TLD. |
| **overboil.gg** | "overboil" | The exact lose condition; `.gg` reads as a game. |
| **simmer.club** | "simmer" | The tension *before* the boil; calm-word irony. |
| **cauldron.club** | "cauldron" | The central object; alchemy/arcane-punk fit. |
| **theboil.io** | "the boil" | Punchy, short, techy `.io`. |
| **boilover.xyz** | "boil-over" | The disaster moment; cheap TLD. |

## Brandable / oblique (cheap `.club` / `.xyz`)

Short, evocative, not literal — easy to say, easy to own:

- **sandwich.club** — oblique and sticky ("knuckle sandwich" / a club sandwich of
  betrayal); the kind of odd one-word `.club` that's memorable and likely cheap.
- `mitts.club` · `potluck.club` (literally a shared pot + luck) · `hotpot.club`
  · `tinder.xyz`-style one-worders (`kindling.club`, `tinderbox.xyz`, `combust.xyz`)

## Notes for choosing

- **Pair the name with the gameplay verb.** `overboil`, `boilover`, `lastcard`,
  `pushyourluck`, `potluck` all *say what you do*.
- **One-word `.club` hacks** (`boiling.club`, `simmer.club`, `cauldron.club`,
  `sandwich.club`) are the cheapest "premium-feeling" option and reinforce the
  "join a group/club" wording from the `group-model` change.
- Grab the matching social handle at the same time; consistency beats the perfect TLD.
