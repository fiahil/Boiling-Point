> **✅ Resolved — 2026-06-02. Archived; kept for reference.**

> The rankings below are the historical shortlist that led to this choice.

Candidate domains for the game (working title *Boiling Point*). Goals: **clever**,
**cheap** (priority TLDs: `.quest`, `.club`, `.cards`, `.xyz` — plus `.com`/`.gg`/`.io`/`.fun`),
and **good** (memorable, on-theme: a boiling cauldron, hidden volatility, push-your-luck,
betrayal).

> Availability/price change constantly — **re-verify at a registrar before committing.**

> sweep; **renewals are higher** (`.xyz` $1→~$11, `.quest` ~$2→~$20, `.fun` $4→~$20,

## Notes for choosing

- **Pair the name with the gameplay verb.** `overboil`, `boilover`, `seethe`, `flinch`,
  `pushyourluck` all *say what you do*.
- **`seethe` / `roil`** are the most original — each means turbulent boiling *and*
  simmering rage/discord, which is the whole game in one word.
- **`.club` reinforces the "join a group/club"** wording from the `group-model` change;
  it's the cheapest "premium-feeling" TLD with good words still open.
- Grab the matching social handle at the same time; consistency beats the perfect TLD.
